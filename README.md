# cybersecurity-awareness-toolkit
Simple and practical cybersecurity awareness resources for individuals and organizations.
Hi, I’m Ayomide 👋  
I am a Cybersecurity Lead passionate about helping individuals and organizations stay safe online.

This repository contains simple, practical, and easy-to-understand cybersecurity awareness materials.
## (Goal)
To make cybersecurity knowledge easy, relatable, and accessible for everyone — even non-technical users.

## What You will Find Here
- Password security guides  
- Phishing awareness examples  
- Social media safety tips  
- Cyber hygiene checklists  
- Simple cybersecurity education content

  ## Who This Is For

- Beginners in cybersecurity  
- Non-technical professionals  
- Small teams and organizations  
- Anyone who wants to stay safe online

  ## Why This Project Matters
Cyber threats are increasing every day, and many people fall victim simply because they are not aware.
This project is my way of contributing to a safer digital space by sharing knowledge in a simple and practical way.

## About Me
I support awareness, organization, and digital safety initiatives.
